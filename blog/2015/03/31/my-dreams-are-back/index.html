My Dreams Are Back

Mar 31st, 2015 10:59 pm

That tingling feeling

Ok so if anyone has ever played Super Mario World then you know about the cape power up and how it lets mario fly. So in my dream I had that cape, and I could fly, but I totally sucked at it. I remember I was flying around Milpitas High School at night with no control whatsoever. When I wanted to land I ended up dive bombing at the ground and I would barely pull uop the cape to spike back up towards the sky. I couldn’t glide or fly all I could do was be blown around by the wind in the rainy sky hoping I wouldn’t die. The entire time I’m have the feeling of flying. My girlfriend says when I dream my heart papulates at a million beats per minute so my heart was probably going nuts during this dream. My friends at school also told me I should go get checked up but they’re just jealous they don’t have cool dreams like I do.

Sliced Eggplant

So I added some sliced eggplant to my spaghetti today and I’ll admit it kinda sucked. I have no idea how to cook eggplant the non Asian way. I could have like fried this eggplant and made eggplant permesan but I was trying to be healthy. So yea today my eggplant sucked.
