Sweet Redemption

Apr 7th, 2015 12:15 am

Asian Basketball Tournament

Last Saturday the Asian American Association hosted a basketball tournament on campus. For some reason I always have a rougue team that’s just a collection of me and my baller friends. Last year we lost to AAA in the regular bracket, fought our way through the losers bracket, and ended up playing 7 games in a row just to lose by 3 in the finals.

This year we killed it though, and as captain of the team, I give all credit to my awesome players. We played through 7 games straight with this crazy team. I guess this post is more for myself because I’m just really proud of my fucking team. CHAMPION PICS. We got Chenyang “The Beast” on the top left who’s both a beast on offense and defense. Then we got Alyssa on the right of him who played nonstop for us for seven games straight. Mad props. Austin and Shin on the right of her who have mad handles and speed. My roomate Kevin in the maroon with his clutch mid range shots. Trae, you all know who’s Trae, with his nasty post up game and stretch three point shots. My point guard Liang with his 90% three point shot, and Joon in the black with his unnecessary yet awesome no look passes. We got Sophie and Grace who filled in for Alyssa when her legs broke from playing seven games in a row. Eric, who if he wasn’t injured he would be beating teams all by himself he’s that crazy. AND ME haha. I’m a fool.

I just feel good about bringing a group of people together that barely know each other, and through basketball, fight their hardest towards a common goal. Also I go have to say, when they kept calling me captain and contratulating me it felt really good.
