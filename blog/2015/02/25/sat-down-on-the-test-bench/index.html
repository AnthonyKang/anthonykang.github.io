Captain Gyro

Its harder to find diversified cuisine here in Indiana but Captain Gyro is an exception. It’s owned by some old dude straight out of Greece and he’s got one of those rotating spools of gyro meat and he shaves that shit straight in to some pita bread and its on point. The sandwich here is has extra vegetables (gotta get that fiber) but its filled with meat. No nonsense homemade gyro’s.
