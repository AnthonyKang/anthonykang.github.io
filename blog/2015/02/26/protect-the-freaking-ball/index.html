Protect the Freaking Ball

Feb 26th, 2015 11:30 pm

I freaking had three episodes of dreams last night but I messed up and didin’t write them down before making breakfast so I only remember one. I’m going to start setting up the tomorrow’s posts so I can just slap them down on the post in the morning.

I’m transferring???

So the only dream I remember involved me getting an email in my Purdue email saying that I’m being transferred to a private local college back home, and I had no choice but to go. When I arrived in my hometown, all my buds from home had been extracted from their respecitve colleges and we were forced to take a placement test. So this isn’t very exciting, but the thing is that I remember the test with such detail. I was doing freaking combinatorics problems, drawing RTL diagrams, writing code, and doing IQ test questions. When I woke up I actually said wtf because at one point I was deriving a formula for a summation on paper, like that’s shit I can’t even do when I’m awake… I’m sure my other dreams were more #action_packed but I had to make breakfast.
